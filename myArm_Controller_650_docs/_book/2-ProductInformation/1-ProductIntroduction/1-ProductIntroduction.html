1. Product Overview

myArm Controller 650


General 6 Degree of Freedom Robot Motion Data Acquisition Device

Product Introduction

The myArm C650 is a highly adaptable general-purpose robotic arm with 6 degrees of freedom, expandable to 7 degrees of freedom to meet a wider range of application needs. As a motion data acquisition device, the robotic arm is equipped with end-effector finger controllers and two smart buttons to make operation more intuitive and precise. With an efficient controller (C stands for Controller), the device can output end-effector coordinates or joint angles at a frequency of 50Hz, suitable for research, development, end-to-end AI data collection, and industrial robot teaching applications.

As an important tool for research and development, the myArm C650 serves as a hub for education and research, dedicated to providing perfect solutions for various data collection tasks. Its high flexibility and modularity enable it to handle various complex operations and tasks, demonstrating outstanding performance and reliability in both educational environments and high-demand industrial applications.

Design Philosophy


The design philosophy of the myArm C650 general-purpose intelligent robotic arm is based on a deep understanding of the current needs in technology education and research fields. Rapid advancements in fields like machine learning and artificial intelligence demand experimental equipment that not only has general functionality but also flexibility and precision. The design aims to meet these complex requirements, providing a solution that is both flexible and precise, adaptable to various application scenarios.

3. Supported Expansion Development

The myArm series robotic arms are highly valuable in the education and research fields, especially in the widely used development environments of Python and ROS (Robot Operating System). These environments provide powerful support, allowing myArm series products to be widely used in tasks such as machine learning, artificial intelligence research, complex motion control, and visual processing.
Python - Provides standardized robot driver libraries, available for download and installation via PyPI
- Supports querying joint angle data, Cartesian coordinate data, servo motor encoder data, motor speed, temperature, current, etc.
- These features make the myArm C650 suitable for basic education and research scenarios, especially in data collection and preliminary robot control.
ROS - Supports both ROS1 and ROS2 versions, providing RVIZ simulation environment support.
- Allows users to display real-time status information of the robotic arm and data acquisition, making C650 suitable for ROS beginners and educational purposes.
Hardware Interfaces - Including IO, USB, etc., convenient for connecting various sensors and actuators.
Software Libraries - Provides rich open-source libraries and APIs to simplify the development process.
System Compatibility - Compatible with Windows, Linux, MacOS, adapting to multiple development environments.
