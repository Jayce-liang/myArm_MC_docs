## 1. Product Overview

##### myArm Controller 650

##### General 6 Degree of Freedom Robot Motion Data Acquisition Device

### Product Introduction

The myArm C650 is a highly adaptable general-purpose robotic arm with 6 degrees of freedom, expandable to 7 degrees of freedom to meet a wider range of application needs. As a motion data acquisition device, the robotic arm is equipped with end-effector finger controllers and two smart buttons to make operation more intuitive and precise. With an efficient controller (C stands for Controller), the device can output end-effector coordinates or joint angles at a frequency of 50Hz, suitable for research, development, end-to-end AI data collection, and industrial robot teaching applications.

As an important tool for research and development, the myArm C650 serves as a hub for education and research, dedicated to providing perfect solutions for various data collection tasks. Its high flexibility and modularity enable it to handle various complex operations and tasks, demonstrating outstanding performance and reliability in both educational environments and high-demand industrial applications.
